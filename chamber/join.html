Chamber of Commerce Membership

Membership Benefits

NP Membership: No fee and basic benefits.

Membership Fee: Free

Benefits:

  • Access to basic events and networking opportunities.
  • Monthly newsletter subscription.
  • Limited access to online resources.

Bronze Membership: Increased benefits with a moderate fee.

Membership Fee: $50 per year

Benefits:

  • All benefits of NP Membership.
  • Exclusive access to premium events.
  • Enhanced networking opportunities.
  • Discounted rates on workshops and training sessions.

Silver Membership: More benefits at a higher cost.

Membership Fee: $100 per year

Benefits:

  • All benefits of Bronze Membership.
  • Priority access to industry reports and market insights.
  • Featured listing in the membership directory.
  • Complimentary entry to major conferences.

Gold Membership: Premium benefits with the highest cost.

Membership Fee: $200 per year

Benefits:

  • All benefits of Silver Membership.
  • VIP access to exclusive events with industry leaders.
  • Personalized business consultation sessions.
  • Complimentary advertising opportunities on the organization's website.
