White Space and Clean Design

IKEA

ikea.com

Whitespace, or empty space between elements, is crucial for web design. In the case of IKEA's website, white space is used to declutter, create visual hierarchy, reduce cognitive load, and ensure a clean and responsive design. Ikea is known for being clean minimalist in its style and products. The website is a way for the company to promote that aesthetic.

Rule of Thirds

Target

target.com

Whitespace, or empty space between elements, is crucial for web design. In the case of Target's website, the Rule of Thirds is applied to create a visually appealing layout. Target uses this design principle to enhance the overall user experience and guide the focus of the audience.

Proximity

Pinterest

pinterest.com

Proximity, the grouping of similar elements, is a key design principle used by Pinterest. By organizing content in close proximity, Pinterest ensures a clear visual hierarchy and improves the user experience. The platform leverages proximity to make content discovery and navigation intuitive.
