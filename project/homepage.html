Welcome to Scoots

Your ultimate destination for unforgettable adventures in Cozumel!

At Scoots, we offer an exhilarating way to experience the breathtaking beauty of Cozumel with our diverse range of rental options. From sleek and nimble Honda motor scooters perfect for zipping through the island's vibrant streets to rugged and reliable side-by-side ATVs designed for off-road adventures, we cater to every explorer's desires. What makes us truly awesome is our commitment to providing hassle-free rental services coupled with our intimate knowledge of Cozumel's hidden gems. Whether you're a local looking for a convenient mode of transportation, a spontaneous tourist eager to explore, or a cruise-based traveler seeking a memorable excursion, we've got you covered. With our user-friendly rental process, top-notch equipment, and friendly customer service, we ensure that your island adventure is not only unforgettable but also effortlessly enjoyable. So, hop on one of our vehicles, soak in the sun-kissed scenery, and let the winds of Cozumel carry you on an unforgettable journey of discovery and delight.

  • Honda Metropolitan (49cc) - 1 person: Perfect for zipping around town solo, the Metropolitan offers easy maneuverability and fuel efficiency.
